Wild Light Arts respectfully acknowledges the Ngambri, Ngunnawal, Ngarigo, and Yuin peoples, the Traditional Custodians of the land on which we operate in Braidwood, New South Wales, and surrounding areas. We recognise the deep connections our First Nations people have to the land, waters, and culture, and we honor their Elders past, present, and emerging. We are privileged to create and share our work on this sacred land, and we pay tribute to the rich histories, stories, and artistic traditions that have been nurtured here for countless generations. We are committed to respecting, preserving, and celebrating the cultural heritage and natural landscapes that inspire our photography and our lives.
